The Mighty Italian Meatballs

The one thing that sets this meatball recipe apart from others? Soak sandwich bread with grated onion. The bread puffs up when cooked, making these meatballs extra soft and juicy.
Plus it adds extra savoury flavour without the need to fry onion separately.
